   - EN: A hockey stick costs $80. There is a 10% discount. How much is saved?
   - FR: Un bâton de hockey coûte 80 $. Il y a un rabais de 10 %. Combien économise-t-on?
   - Choices: A) $6  B) $8  C) $10  D) $12
   - Hint: 10% means divide by 10.
   - Steps:
     - 10% of $80 = $80 ÷ 10.
     - Savings = $8.
   - Answer: 8
