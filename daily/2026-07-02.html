  2. [Easy] Combinations

    • FR: De combien de façons peut-on choisir 2 garnitures parmi 5 options?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 10 C) 20 D) 25

    • Hint: For f(x) = x², f'(x) = 2x; at x = 1: slope = 2, f(1) = 1; tangent: y − 1 = 2(x − 1) → y = 2x − 1.
    • Steps:

  10. [Hard] Probability Distribution — Expected Value

    • EN: A game pays $10 if you roll a 6 on a standard die, $0 otherwise. You pay $2 to play. What is the expected value of the game (net)?
    • FR: Un jeu paie 10 $ si tu lances un 6 avec un dé standard, 0 $ sinon. Tu paies 2 $ pour jouer. Quelle est la valeur espérée nette?
    • Choices: A) −$0.33 B) $0.33 C) −$1.33 D) $1.67
    • Hint: E(X) = Σ[x × P(x)]; for winning $8 with prob 1/6 and losing $2 with prob 5/6: E = 8/6 − 10/6 = −2/6 ≈ −$0.33.

    • Answer: −$0.33
