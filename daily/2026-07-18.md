    - Choices: A) y = x − 3  B) y = 2x − 6  C) y = 2x − 5  D) y = 4x − 9
    - Hint: Find the derivative for the slope, then use the point-slope form y − y₁ = m(x − x₁) — like f(x) = x², at x = 2: slope = 4, point = (2, 4), tangent: y = 4x − 4.
    - Steps:
      - f'(x) = 2x − 4. At x = 3: slope = 2(3) − 4 = 2.
      - f(3) = 9 − 12 + 3 = 0. Point = (3, 0).

## Mini Story of Kindness

Amara lived in Charlottetown and loved spending Saturdays at the community garden on Prince Street. One afternoon, she noticed her elderly neighbour Mr. Leblanc struggling to carry a heavy bag of soil to his garden plot. Without being asked, Amara grabbed one handle and helped him carry it all the way across the garden. Mr. Leblanc smiled and taught her how to plant tomatoes in a perfect row — and Amara discovered that even kindness has a pattern to it.
