Daily Math — 2026-04-30

Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Sleds

    • EN: There are 5 red sleds and 4 blue sleds. How many sleds are there in total?
    • FR: Il y a 5 luges rouges et 4 luges bleues. Combien de luges y a-t-il au total?
    • Choices: A) 6 B) 9 C) 8 D) 7
    • Hint: Add the two numbers together.
    • Steps:
      • Count red sleds.
      • Count blue sleds.
    • Answer: 9

  2. [Easy] Hockey Goals

    • EN: Emma scored 3 goals in one game and 2 in another. How many goals did she score in total?
    • FR: Emma a marqué 3 buts lors d'un match et 2 lors d'un autre. Combien de buts a-t-elle marqués au total?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 4 C) 6 D) 7
    • Hint: Add the goals together.
    • Steps:
      • Count goals from the first game.
      • Count goals from the second game.
    • Answer: 5

  3. [Easy] Maple Syrup Bottles

    • EN: There are 10 bottles of maple syrup on the table. If 2 are empty, how many are full?
    • FR: Il y a 10 bouteilles de sirop d'érable sur la table. Si 2 sont vides, combien sont pleines?
    • Choices: A) 8 B) 9 C) 7 D) 6
    • Hint: Subtract the empty bottles from the total.
    • Steps:
      • Find the total number of bottles.
      • Subtract the empty ones.
    • Answer: 8

  4. [Easy] Coin Count

    • EN: If you have 5 nickels and 3 dimes, how many coins do you have?
    • FR: Si tu as 5 sous et 3 dimes, combien de pièces as-tu?
    • Choices: A) 6 B) 7 C) 8 D) 9
    • Hint: Count all the coins together.
    • Steps:
      • Count nickels.
      • Count dimes.
    • Answer: 8

  5. [Medium] Adding Up Volumes

    • EN: A box has a volume of 3 cubic meters and another box has a volume of 5 cubic meters. What is the total volume?
    • FR: Une boîte a un volume de 3 mètres cubes et une autre boîte a un volume de 5 mètres cubes. Quel est le volume total?
    • Choices: A) 6 B) 7 C) 8 D) 9
    • Hint: Think about how to combine the volumes.
    • Steps:
      • Add the volumes of both boxes.
    • Answer: 8

  6. [Medium] Neighbours' Ages

    • EN: If Mia is 7 years old and her brother is 2 years younger, how old is her brother?
    • FR: Si Mia a 7 ans et que son frère a 2 ans de moins, quel âge a son frère?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 4 C) 6 D) 3
    • Hint: Subtract 2 from Mia's age.
    • Steps:
      • Find Mia's age.
      • Subtract 2 years.
    • Answer: 5

  7. [Medium] Fruit Basket

    • EN: In a basket, there are 6 apples and 4 oranges. How many fruits are there in total?
    • FR: Dans un panier, il y a 6 pommes et 4 oranges. Combien de fruits y a-t-il au total?
    • Choices: A) 9 B) 10 C) 11 D) 8
    • Hint: Combine the number of apples and oranges.
    • Steps:
      • Count apples.
      • Count oranges.
    • Answer: 10

  8. [Medium] Snowfall Yesterday

    • EN: Yesterday, it snowed 2 cm in the morning and 3 cm in the afternoon. How much snow fell in total?
    • FR: Hier, il a neigé 2 cm le matin et 3 cm l’après-midi. Combien de neige est tombée au total?
    • Choices: A) 4 B) 5 C) 6 D) 7
    • Hint: Add the two amounts of snow.
    • Steps:
      • Measure snow in the morning.
      • Measure snow in the afternoon.
    • Answer: 5

  9. [Hard] Comparing Distances

    • EN: A truck travels 50 km in 1 hour and 30 minutes, while a car travels 65 km in 1 hour. Which vehicle travels farther?
    • FR: Un camion parcourt 50 km en 1 heure et 30 minutes, tandis qu'une voiture parcourt 65 km en 1 heure. Quel véhicule parcourt le plus de distance?
    • Choices: A) Truck B) Car C) Both D) None
    • Hint: Compare the distances covered in the same time.
    • Steps:
      • Convert travel times to hours.
      • Calculate average speed and compare.
    • Answer: Car

  10. [Hard] Time Calculation

    • EN: If the movie starts at 6:15 PM and is 2 hours and 45 minutes long, what time will it end?
    • FR: Si le film commence à 18h15 et dure 2 heures et 45 minutes, à quelle heure se terminera-t-il?
    • Choices: A) 8:00 PM B) 9:00 PM C) 9:15 PM D) 8:30 PM
    • Hint: Add the total duration to the start time.
    • Steps:
      • Calculate total hours.
      • Add to start time.
    • Answer: 9:00 PM

Grade 2 Problems

  1. [Easy] Adding Coins

    • EN: You have 3 pennies and 2 nickels. How many coins do you have in total?
    • FR: Tu as 3 pennies et 2 nickels. Combien de pièces as-tu au total?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 4 C) 6 D) 3
    • Hint: Count all the coins together.
    • Steps:
      • Count pennies.
      • Count nickels.
    • Answer: 5

  2. [Easy] Clock Reading

    • EN: What time is it if the hour hand is on 2 and the minute hand is on 6?
    • FR: Quelle heure est-il si la grande aiguille est sur 6 et la petite aiguille est sur 2?
    • Choices: A) 2:30 B) 2:15 C) 3:00 D) 1:30
    • Hint: Think about how minutes correspond to numbers on the clock.
    • Steps:
      • Identify hour.
      • Identify minute.
    • Answer: 2:30

  3. [Easy] Length of Pencil

    • EN: Your pencil is 10 cm long. If you sharpen it and make it 2 cm shorter, how long is it now?
    • FR: Ton crayon mesure 10 cm de long. Si tu le tailles et qu'il mesure 2 cm de moins, quelle est sa longueur maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 8 B) 9 C) 10 D) 7
    • Hint: Subtract the length it was shortened.
    • Steps:
      • Start with original length.
      • Subtract 2 cm.
    • Answer: 8

  4. [Easy] Playground Fun

    • EN: There are 4 swings and 3 slides in the playground. How many play items are there in total?
    • FR: Il y a 4 balançoires et 3 glissières dans le terrain de jeu. Combien d'éléments de jeu y a-t-il au total?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 7 C) 6 D) 8
    • Hint: Add the swings and slides.
    • Steps:
      • Count swings.
      • Count slides.
    • Answer: 7

  5. [Medium] Coin Counting

    • EN: You have 20 cents in nickels and dimes. If you have 2 dimes, how many nickels do you have?
    • FR: Tu as 20 cents en nickels et en dimes. Si tu as 2 dimes, combien de nickels as-tu?
    • Choices: A) 2 B) 4 C) 6 D) 8
    • Hint: Convert dimes to cents and subtract from the total.
    • Steps:
      • Calculate value of dimes.
      • Subtract from total to find nickels.
    • Answer: 4

  6. [Medium] Cooking Time

    • EN: A recipe takes 1 hour and 15 minutes to cook. If you start at 5 PM, what time will it be done?
    • FR: Une recette prend 1 heure et 15 minutes à cuire. Si tu commences à 17h, à quelle heure sera-t-elle prête?
    • Choices: A) 6:00 PM B) 6:15 PM C) 6:30 PM D) 5:45 PM
    • Hint: Add the cooking time to the start time.
    • Steps:
      • Find total cooking time.
      • Add to starting time.
    • Answer: 6:15 PM

  7. [Medium] Length of Rope

    • EN: A rope is 2 meters long. If you cut it into 4 equal pieces, what is the length of each piece?
    • FR: Une corde mesure 2 mètres de long. Si tu la coupes en 4 morceaux égaux, quelle est la longueur de chaque morceau?
    • Choices: A) 0.5 B) 0.75 C) 0.25 D) 0.4
    • Hint: Think about dividing into equal parts.
    • Steps:
      • Divide total length by number of pieces.
    • Answer: 0.5

  8. [Medium] Bouncing Ball

    • EN: A ball bounces 5 times. If it goes up 2 meters each time, how high does it go in total?
    • FR: Une balle rebondit 5 fois. Si elle monte de 2 mètres à chaque fois, quelle est la hauteur totale qu'elle atteint?
    • Choices: A) 7 B) 10 C) 12 D) 15
    • Hint: How would you find the total height for multiple bounces?
    • Steps:
      • Calculate height for each bounce.
      • Multiply by the number of bounces.
    • Answer: 10

  9. [Hard] Buying School Supplies

    • EN: You buy 3 notebooks for $2 each and 2 packs of pencils for $3 each. How much did you spend in total?
    • FR: Tu achètes 3 cahiers à 2 $ chacun et 2 paquets de crayons à 3 $ chacun. Combien as-tu dépensé au total?
    • Choices: A) 12 B) 11 C) 9 D) 10
    • Hint: Calculate each item's cost and add them together.
    • Steps:
      • Calculate total for notebooks.
      • Calculate total for pencils.
    • Answer: 12

  10. [Hard] Patterning Fruits

    • EN: A fruit shop sells apples in groups of 3, and you buy 6 groups. How many apples do you have in total?
    • FR: Un magasin de fruits vend des pommes par groupes de 3 et tu achètes 6 groupes. Combien de pommes as-tu au total?
    • Choices: A) 15 B) 18 C) 21 D) 24
    • Hint: Multiply the number of groups by apples in each group.
    • Steps:
      • Multiply groups by apples per group.
      • Answer: 18
