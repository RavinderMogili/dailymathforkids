    • EN: What is f(2) if f(x) = x² + x + 2?
    • FR: Quelle est f(2) si f(x) = x² + x + 2 ?
    • Choices: A) 4 B) 6 C) 8 D) 10
    • Hint: Substitute x with 2 in the function.
    • Steps:
      • Evaluate the expression
    • Answer: 8

  2. [Easy] Finding Roots

    • EN: What are the roots of the equation x² - 4 = 0?
    • FR: Quelles sont les racines de l'équation x² - 4 = 0 ?
    • Choices: A) ±2 B) ±1 C) ±3 D) ±4
    • Hint: Factor the expression if possible.
    • Steps:
      • Solve for x value
    • Answer: ±2

  3. [Easy] Identifying Linear Functions

    • EN: What is the slope of the line described by y = 3x + 4?
    • FR: Quelle est la pente de la droite décrite par y = 3x + 4 ?
    • Choices: A) 1 B) 2 C) 3 D) 4
    • Hint: The coefficient of x gives the slope.
    • Steps:
      • Identify the coefficient
    • Answer: 3

  4. [Easy] Factoring Polynomials

    • EN: Factor the polynomial x² + 3x + 2.
    • FR: Facteur le polynôme x² + 3x + 2.
    • Choices: A) (x + 2)(x + 1) B) (x + 3)(x + 2) C) (x + 1)(x + 3) D) (x + 4)(x + 1)
    • Hint: Look for two numbers that multiply to +2 and add to +3.
    • Steps:
      • Identify factors
    • Answer: (x + 1)(x + 2)

  5. [Medium] Solving Linear Equations

    • EN: Solve for x: 2(x - 3) = 14.
    • FR: Résoudre pour x : 2(x - 3) = 14.
    • Choices: A) 10 B) 9 C) 11 D) 12
    • Hint: Distribute first, then solve for x.
    • Steps:
      • Simplify and isolate x
    • Answer: 10
