    - Hint: P(A|B) = P(A and B) / P(B) — like if P(A and B) = 0.3 and P(B) = 0.6, then P(A|B) = 0.5.
    - Steps:
      - P(math and science) = 0.40. P(math) = 0.60.
      - P(science | math) = 0.40 / 0.60 = 2/3.
      - The probability is 2/3.
    - Answer: 2/3
