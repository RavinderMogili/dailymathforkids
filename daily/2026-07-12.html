Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Carrots

    • EN: Mia has 7 carrots. She picks 5 more. How many carrots does she have now?
    • FR: Mia a 7 carottes. Elle en cueille 5 de plus. Combien de carottes a-t-elle maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 11 B) 12 C) 13 D) 14
    • Hint: To add, count on from the bigger number — 9 + 3, start at 9 and count up 3.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 7.
      • Count on 5 more: 8, 9, 10, 11, 12.
      • So Mia has 12 carrots.
    • Answer: 12

  2. [Easy] Hockey Pucks

      • Tens: 3 + 2 = 5.
      • So 34 + 25 = 59.

  5. [Medium] Subtracting Within 100

    • EN: Omar scored 72 points in a game. He lost 38 points. How many points does he have now?
    • FR: Omar a marqué 72 points dans un jeu. Il en a perdu 38. Combien de points a-t-il maintenant?

      • Ones: 2 − 8, need to regroup; borrow 1 ten, now 12 − 8 = 4.
      • Tens: 6 − 3 = 3 (since we used one ten).
      • So 72 − 38 = 34.
    • Answer: 34
