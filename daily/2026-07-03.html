  1. [Easy] Permutations

    • FR: De combien de façons peut-on placer 4 étudiants en ligne?
    • Choices: A) 16 B) 20 C) 24 D) 28
    • Hint: The number of arrangements of n items is n! (n factorial).
    • Steps:
      • 4! = 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 24.
    • Answer: 24

  2. [Easy] Combinations

    • EN: How many ways can you choose 2 toppings from 5 options for a Tim Hortons bagel?
    • FR: De combien de façons peut-on choisir 2 garnitures parmi 5 options pour un bagel Tim Hortons?
    • Choices: A) 8 B) 10 C) 12 D) 20
    • Hint: Use C(n, r) = n! / (r! × (n−r)!).
    • Steps:
      • C(5, 2) = 5! / (2! × 3!) = (5 × 4) / (2 × 1) = 10.
    • Answer: 10

  3. [Easy] Derivative (Rate of Change)

    • EN: If f(x) = 3x², what is f'(x)?
    • FR: Si f(x) = 3x², que vaut f'(x)?
    • Choices: A) 3x B) 6x C) 3x² D) 6x²
    • Hint: Use the power rule: d/dx [axⁿ] = n·axⁿ⁻¹.
    • Steps:
      • Bring down the power: 2 × 3 = 6.
      • Decrease the power by 1: x².
      • f'(x) = 6x.
    • Answer: 6x

  4. [Easy] Polynomial Function — Zeros

    • EN: What are the zeros of f(x) = (x − 2)(x + 5)?
    • FR: Quels sont les zéros de f(x) = (x − 2)(x + 5)?

    • Hint: Set each factor equal to zero and solve for x.
    • Steps:
      • x − 2 = 0 → x = 2.
      • x + 5 = 0 → x = −5.
    • Answer: x = 2 and x = −5

  5. [Medium] Binomial Probability

    • EN: A hockey player has a 70% chance of scoring on each penalty shot. What is the probability that she scores on exactly 2 of 3 shots?
    • FR: Une joueuse de hockey a 70 % de chances de marquer à chaque tir de pénalité. Quelle est la probabilité qu'elle marque exactement 2 fois sur 3 tirs?

    • Hint: Use P(X = k) = C(n, k) × pᵏ × (1−p)ⁿ⁻ᵏ.

  6. [Medium] Derivative — Tangent Slope

    • EN: Find the slope of the tangent to f(x) = x³ − 4x at x = 2.
