    - Hint: Use the binomial formula P = C(n, k) × pᵏ × (1 − p)ⁿ⁻ᵏ. For 3 flips, exactly 2 heads: C(3,2) × (0.5)² × (0.5)¹ = 3/8.
    - Steps:
      - P = C(4, 3) × (0.5)³ × (0.5)¹.
      - = 4 × (1/8) × (1/2) = 4/16 = 1/4.
      - The probability is 1/4.
    - Answer: 1/4

## Mini Story of Kindness

Amara had just finished her skating lesson at the rink in Yellowknife when she noticed a younger boy sitting alone, crying near the boards. His laces had come undone and he did not know how to retie his skates. Amara skated over, knelt down, and carefully tied them into a strong double knot, showing him each step slowly. The boy smiled and wobbled back onto the ice, and Amara felt a warm glow that was better than any goal she had ever scored.
