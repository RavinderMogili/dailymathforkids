  2. [Easy] Derivative Intro — Power Rule

    • EN: If f(x) = x³, what is f'(x)?
    • FR: Si f(x) = x³, combien vaut f'(x) ?
    • Choices: A) x² B) 2x² C) 3x² D) 3x³
    • Hint: Power rule: d/dx(xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹ — like d/dx(x⁴) = 4x³.
    • Steps:
      • Bring down the exponent: 3.
      • Reduce power by 1: x². So f'(x) = 3x².
    • Answer: 3x²

  3. [Easy] Combinations

    • EN: How many ways can you choose 2 students from a group of 5?
    • FR: De combien de façons peut-on choisir 2 élèves parmi un groupe de 5 ?
    • Choices: A) 8 B) 10 C) 12 D) 20
    • Hint: C(n,r) = n!/(r!(n−r)!) — like C(4,2) = 4!/(2!×2!) = 6.
    • Steps:
      • C(5,2) = 5!/(2! × 3!) = (5 × 4)/(2 × 1).
      • = 20 ÷ 2 = 10.
    • Answer: 10
