Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Apples

    • EN: There are 7 apples on the table and 5 more in a basket. How many apples are there in all?
    • FR: Il y a 7 pommes sur la table et 5 de plus dans un panier. Combien y a-t-il de pommes en tout?

    • Steps:
      • Start at 7 and count on 5 more.
      • 8, 9, 10, 11, 12 — so there are 12 apples in all.
    • Answer: 12
