  5. [Medium] Long Division with Remainder

    • EN: 53 campers are put into groups of 6. How many full groups are made, and how many campers are left over?
    • FR: 53 campeurs sont répartis en groupes de 6. Combien de groupes complets y a-t-il, et combien de campeurs restent-ils?

    • Steps:

      • 9/12 > 8/12, so 3/4 is greater. Its numerator is 3.
    • Answer: 3
