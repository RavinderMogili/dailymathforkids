   - EN: What is 30% of 200?
   - FR: Combien font 30 % de 200 ?
   - Choices: A) 50  B) 60  C) 30  D) 80
   - Hint: 30% = 30/100. Multiply 200 × 30 ÷ 100.
   - Steps:
     - 200 × 30 = 6000.
     - 6000 ÷ 100 = 60.
   - Answer: 60

2. **[Easy] Order of Operations (BEDMAS)**
   - EN: Evaluate: 3 + 4 × 2.
   - FR: Évalue : 3 + 4 × 2.
   - Choices: A) 14  B) 10  C) 11  D) 8
   - Hint: Multiplication comes before addition (BEDMAS).
   - Steps:
     - 4 × 2 = 8 first.
     - 3 + 8 = 11.
   - Answer: 11
