Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Loonies

    • EN: How many loonies are there in 3 dollars?
    • FR: Combien de dollars y a-t-il dans 3 pièces de 1 dollar?
    • Choices: A) 4 B) 3 C) 5 D) 2
    • Hint: Think how many 1-dollar coins make up 3 dollars.
    • Steps:
      • Count the total dollars.
      • Each loonie is worth 1 dollar.
    • Answer: 3

  2. [Easy] Add the Dimes

    • EN: You have 2 dimes. How much money do you have?
    • FR: Tu as 2 dimes. Combien d'argent as-tu?
    • Choices: A) 15 cents B) 20 cents C) 30 cents D) 10 cents
    • Hint: Each dime is worth 10 cents.
    • Steps:
      • Find the value of one dime.
      • Multiply by 2.
    • Answer: 20

  3. [Easy] Skip Count by 2s

    • EN: What comes after 4 when you skip count by 2s?
    • FR: Quel nombre vient après 4 quand tu comptes par 2?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 6 C) 7 D) 8
    • Hint: Add 2 to 4.
    • Steps:
      • Start from 4.
      • Increase by 2.
    • Answer: 6

  4. [Easy] Subtracting Apples

    • Hint: Subtract the eaten apples from total.
    • Steps:
      • Start with 10.
      • Take away 3.

  7. [Medium] Total Pennies
