    • Hint: Write area as A = w × l, where 2w + l = total fencing; express A in terms of one variable, differentiate, and set equal to zero.
    • Steps:
      • Let width = w and length = l. Constraint: 2w + l = 60, so l = 60 − 2w.
      • Area: A(w) = w(60 − 2w) = 60w − 2w².
      • Differentiate: A'(w) = 60 − 4w. Set A'(w) = 0: 60 − 4w = 0 → w = 15.
      • The width that maximizes area is 15 m.
    • Answer: 15 m
