Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Hockey Pucks

    • EN: There are 7 hockey pucks on the ice. 3 more are added. How many pucks are there now?
    • FR: Il y a 7 rondelles de hockey sur la glace. On en ajoute 3 de plus. Combien de rondelles y a-t-il maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 9 pucks B) 10 pucks C) 11 pucks D) 8 pucks
    • Hint: Count on from the bigger number. For 5 + 2, start at 5 and count up 2 more.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 7 and count on 3 more.
      • 7 → 8, 9, 10.
      • So there are 10 pucks.
    • Answer: 10 pucks

  2. [Easy] Skip Counting by 5s

    • EN: Skip count by 5s. What comes after 15?
    • FR: Compte par bonds de 5. Quel nombre vient après 15?

    • Hint: When you skip count by 5s, add 5 each time. After 10 comes 15.
    • Steps:
      • We are counting by 5s: 5, 10, 15, …
      • Add 5 to 15: 15 + 5 = 20.
      • So the next number is 20.
    • Answer: 20
