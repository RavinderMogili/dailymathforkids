Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Hockey Pucks

    • EN: There are 7 hockey pucks on the ice. 3 more are added. How many pucks are there now?
    • FR: Il y a 7 rondelles de hockey sur la glace. On en ajoute 3 de plus. Combien y a-t-il de rondelles maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 9 B) 10 C) 11 D) 8
    • Hint: Count on from 7 using your fingers.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 7.
      • Count 3 more: 8, 9, 10.
    • Answer: 10

  2. [Easy] Maple Leaf Counting

    • EN: Emma picks 5 maple leaves. Then she picks 4 more. How many leaves does she have in all?
    • FR: Emma cueille 5 feuilles d'érable. Puis elle en cueille 4 de plus. Combien de feuilles a-t-elle en tout?
    • Choices: A) 8 B) 7 C) 9 D) 10
    • Hint: Add the two groups of leaves together.
    • Steps:
      • Start with 5 leaves.
      • Add 4 more: 5 + 4 = 9.
    • Answer: 9

  3. [Easy] Pennies in a Jar

    • EN: Liam has 6 pennies. He gives 2 to his friend. How many pennies does Liam have left?
    • FR: Liam a 6 sous. Il en donne 2 à son ami. Combien de sous lui reste-t-il?
    • Choices: A) 5 B) 3 C) 4 D) 8
    • Hint: Take away the pennies he gave away.
    • Steps:
      • Start with 6 pennies.
      • Subtract 2: 6 − 2 = 4.
    • Answer: 4

  4. [Easy] Skip Counting by 2s

    • EN: Skip count by 2s. What comes after 8, 10, 12, ___?
    • FR: Compte par bonds de 2. Quel nombre vient après 8, 10, 12, ___?
    • Choices: A) 13 B) 15 C) 14 D) 16
    • Hint: Add 2 to the last number.
    • Steps:
      • The pattern goes up by 2 each time.
      • 12 + 2 = 14.
    • Answer: 14

  5. [Medium] Snowflakes on a Window

    • FR: Il y a 14 flocons de neige sur une fenêtre. 6 fondent. Combien en reste-t-il?

    • Hint: Subtract the ones that melted from the total.
    • Steps:
      • Start with 14 snowflakes.
      • Subtract 6: 14 − 6 = 8.
    • Answer: 8

  6. [Medium] Tim Hortons Timbits

    • EN: A box has 10 Timbits. Sofia eats 3 and her brother eats 2. How many Timbits are left?
    • FR: Une boîte contient 10 Timbits. Sofia en mange 3 et son frère en mange 2. Combien de Timbits reste-t-il?

    • Hint: First figure out how many were eaten in total.
    • Steps:
      • Sofia and her brother ate 3 + 2 = 5 Timbits.
      • 10 − 5 = 5 left.
    • Answer: 5

  7. [Medium] Skip Counting by 5s

    • EN: Count by 5s. What is the missing number? 5, 10, 15, ___, 25

  8. [Medium] Comparing Numbers

    • FR: Quel nombre est le plus grand : 13 ou 17?

    • Hint: Think about which number you reach later when counting.

  9. [Hard] Coins at the Store

    • EN: Ava has 2 nickels and 3 pennies. How many cents does she have in all?

Grade 2 Problems

  1. [Easy] Adding at the Campsite

    • EN: There are 23 campers at Algonquin Park. 14 more arrive. How many campers are there now?
    • FR: Il y a 23 campeurs au parc Algonquin. 14 de plus arrivent. Combien y a-t-il de campeurs maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 36 B) 37 C) 38 D) 35
    • Hint: Add the tens first, then the ones.
    • Steps:
      • 23 + 14: Add tens: 20 + 10 = 30.
      • Add ones: 3 + 4 = 7. Total: 37.
    • Answer: 37

  2. [Easy] Telling Time

    • EN: The clock shows 3 o'clock. Hockey practice starts in half an hour. What time does practice start?
    • FR: L'horloge montre 3 heures. L'entraînement de hockey commence dans une demi-heure. À quelle heure commence l'entraînement?
    • Choices: A) 3:00 B) 4:00 C) 3:30 D) 2:30
    • Hint: Half an hour after 3:00 means the minute hand moves to 6.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 3:00.
      • Add 30 minutes: 3:30.
    • Answer: 3.30

  3. [Easy] Measuring a Pencil

    • EN: A pencil is 15 cm long. A eraser is 3 cm long. How much longer is the pencil than the eraser?
    • FR: Un crayon mesure 15 cm de long. Une gomme mesure 3 cm de long. De combien le crayon est-il plus long que la gomme?

    • Hint: Subtract the shorter length from the longer one.
    • Steps:
      • 15 − 3 = 12 cm.
      • The pencil is 12 cm longer.
    • Answer: 12

  4. [Easy] Counting Coins

  7. [Medium] Snowfall Measurement

    • Choices: A) 42 B) 44 C) 43 D) 45
    • Hint: Add the two snowfall amounts together.
    • Steps:
      • 24 + 19: Add ones: 4 + 9 = 13, write 3 carry 1.
      • Add tens: 2 + 1 + 1 = 4. Answer: 43.

  8. [Medium] Time on the Clock

    • EN: School ends at 3:30. Ben takes 30 minutes to walk home. What time does he arrive?
    • FR: L'école se termine à 15h30. Ben prend 30 minutes pour rentrer à pied. À quelle heure arrive-t-il?

  9. [Hard] Money Problem

    • EN: Jake wants a book that costs 90 cents. He has 4 dimes and 3 nickels. How many more cents does he need?
    • FR: Jake veut un livre qui coûte 90 cents. Il a 4 pièces de 10 cents et 3 pièces de 5 cents. Combien de cents de plus lui faut-il?

  10. [Hard] Length Problem

    • EN: A ribbon is 1 metre long. Chloe cuts off 37 cm. How many centimetres of ribbon are left?
    • FR: Un ruban mesure 1 mètre de long. Chloé coupe 37 cm. Combien de centimètres de ruban reste-t-il?

  3. [Easy] Area of a Rectangle

    • FR: Quelle fraction est la plus grande : 3/4 ou 1/4?
