Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting to 20

    • EN: There are 7 apples on the table and 5 more apples in the basket. How many apples are there in all?
    • FR: Il y a 7 pommes sur la table et 5 autres pommes dans le panier. Combien y a-t-il de pommes en tout?
    • Choices: A) 10 B) 11 C) 12 D) 13
    • Hint: To add 4 + 3, count on from the bigger number: 4 … 5, 6, 7. That's 7.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 7 and count on 5 more.
      • 7 … 8, 9, 10, 11, 12.
      • So there are 12 apples in all.
    • Answer: 12
