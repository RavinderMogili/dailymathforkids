## Mini Story of Kindness

Amara was helping at her family's honey stand at the Saskatoon farmers' market when she noticed an elderly man struggling to read the handwritten price signs. She politely walked over and read every label aloud, explaining the difference between wildflower and clover honey. The man smiled warmly and said it was the most helpful thing anyone had done for him all week. Amara's mother put a small jar aside for him as a gift, and Amara felt proud that a small act of kindness had made such a big difference.
