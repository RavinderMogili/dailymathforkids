Sample Problems (Grade 1–12 preview)

  1. Loonie Count
    EN: Maya has 7 loonies. She finds 5 more under the couch. How many does she have now?
    FR: Maya a 7 pièces d'un dollar. Elle en trouve 5 autres sous le canapé. Combien en a-t-elle maintenant?
    Grade Tag: G1
    Show hint

    Count all loonies together: 7 + 5

    Show steps
    • Start with 7 loonies
    • Count on 5 more: 8, 9, 10, 11, 12
    • Answer: 12
  2. Hockey Cards
    EN: Liam has 48 hockey cards. He gives 19 to his friend. How many does he have left?
    FR: Liam a 48 cartes de hockey. Il en donne 19 à son ami. Combien lui en reste-t-il?
    Grade Tag: G2
    Show hint

    Subtract: 48 − 19

    Show steps
    • 48 − 19 = ?
    • 48 − 20 = 28, then +1 = 29
  3. Maple Syrup Bottles
    EN: A farm fills 6 boxes with 8 bottles of maple syrup each. How many bottles in total?
    FR: Une ferme remplit 6 boîtes avec 8 bouteilles de sirop d'érable chacune. Combien y a-t-il de bouteilles en tout?
    Grade Tag: G3
    Show hint

    Multiply: 6 × 8

    Show steps
    • 6 groups of 8
    • 6 × 8 = 48
  4. Tim Hortons Discount
    EN: A Tim Hortons muffin costs $2.40. There is a 25% discount. What is the sale price?
    FR: Un muffin Tim Hortons coûte 2,40 $. Il y a un rabais de 25 %. Quel est le prix de vente?
    Grade Tag: G6
    Show hint

    Find 25% of $2.40, then subtract from the original price.

    Show steps
    • 25% of $2.40 = $2.40 ÷ 4 = $0.60
    • $2.40 − $0.60 = $1.80
  5. Snowfall Equation
    EN: It snowed x cm on Monday and 14 cm on Tuesday. Total snowfall was 29 cm. Find x.
    FR: Il a neigé x cm lundi et 14 cm mardi. La chute de neige totale était de 29 cm. Trouvez x.
    Grade Tag: G8
    Show hint

    Write an equation: x + 14 = 29

    Show steps
    • x + 14 = 29
    • x = 29 − 14
    • x = 15
