Grade 1 Problems

  1. Loonie Math

    • EN: Maya has 4 loonies. She finds 3 more. How many loonies does she have?
    • FR: Maya a 4 huards. Elle en trouve 3 autres. Combien a-t-elle de huards?
    • Hint: Count all the loonies together.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 4
      • Count 3 more
    • Answer: 7

  2. Snowflakes

    • EN: There are 8 snowflakes on a mitten. 5 melt away. How many are left?
    • FR: Il y a 8 flocons sur une mitaine. 5 fondent. Combien en reste-t-il?
    • Hint: Take away 5 from 8.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 8
      • Subtract 5
    • Answer: 3

  3. Hockey Goals

    • EN: Ben scored 2 goals on Monday and 3 goals on Tuesday. How many in total?
    • FR: Ben a marqué 2 buts lundi et 3 mardi. Combien en tout?
    • Hint: Add the goals together.
    • Steps:
      • 2 goals + 3 goals
    • Answer: 5

  4. Apples

    • EN: There are 10 apples in a basket. Sam takes 4. How many are left?
    • FR: Il y a 10 pommes dans un panier. Sam en prend 4. Combien en reste-t-il?
    • Hint: Subtract Sam's apples.
    • Steps:
      • 10 - 4 = ?
    • Answer: 6

  5. Toonies

    • EN: Ava has 5 toonies. She spends 2. How many toonies does she have left?
    • FR: Ava a 5 pièces de 2 $. Elle en dépense 2. Combien lui en reste-t-il?
    • Hint: Subtract the ones she spent.
    • Steps:
      • 5 - 2 = ?
    • Answer: 3

Grade 6 Problems

  1. Percent

    • EN: A $40 hoodie is on sale for 25% off. How much is the discount?
    • FR: Un chandail à 40 $ est en solde à 25 % de rabais. Quel est le rabais?
    • Hint: Find 25% of $40.
    • Steps:
      • 40 × 0.25 = ?
    • Answer: 10

  2. Ratio

    • EN: In a class of 30 students, 18 play hockey. Write the ratio of hockey players to total students in simplest form.
    • FR: Dans une classe de 30, 18 jouent au hockey. Écris le ratio simplifié.
    • Hint: Simplify 18:30 by dividing both by 6.
    • Steps:
      • 18 ÷ 6 = 3, 30 ÷ 6 = 5
    • Answer: 0.6

  3. Integers

    • EN: The temperature is -5°C in the morning and rises 9°C by noon. What is noon temperature?
    • FR: La température est -5 °C le matin et monte de 9 °C. Quelle est la température à midi?
    • Hint: Add 9 to -5.
    • Steps:
      • -5 + 9 = ?
    • Answer: 4

  4. BEDMAS

    • EN: Solve: 3 + 4 × 2
    • FR: Résous: 3 + 4 × 2
    • Hint: Multiplication before addition (BEDMAS).
    • Steps:
      • 4 × 2 = 8, then 3 + 8 = ?
    • Answer: 11

  5. Rate

    • EN: A car travels 120 km in 2 hours. What is its speed in km/h?
    • FR: Une voiture parcourt 120 km en 2 heures. Quelle est sa vitesse en km/h?
    • Hint: Divide distance by time.
    • Steps:
      • 120 ÷ 2 = ?
    • Answer: 60

    • Steps:
      • 3x^(3-1) = ?

      • 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = ?

    • FR: De combien de façons peut-on choisir 2 élèves parmi 5?
    • Hint: C(5,2) = 5! / (2! × 3!)
    • Steps:
      • 10
    • Answer: 10

    • Hint: Factor the numerator.
    • Steps:
      • (x+2)(x-2)/(x-2) = x+2, at x=2 = ?
