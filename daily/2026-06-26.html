    • Steps:
      • Step 1: Try 3/7 + 2/7. Add numerators: 3 + 2 = 5. Keep denominator: 5/7.
      • Step 2: Only add the top numbers (numerators). Never change the bottom number (denominator)!

    • Hint: 25% = 1/4. To find 25% of a number, divide by 4! For example, 25% of 60 = 60 ÷ 4 = $15 discount.
    • Steps:

      • Step 2: 25% is always one-quarter. Divide the price by 4!
    • Answer: $20

    • EN: What is 3 + 4 × 2?
    • FR: Combien font 3 + 4 × 2 ?

    • Hint: In BEDMAS, multiplication comes before addition! For example, 5 + 3 × 4: do 3 × 4 = 12 first, then 5 + 12 = 17.
    • Steps:
      • Step 1: Try 6 + 2 × 5. Multiply first: 2 × 5 = 10. Then add: 6 + 10 = 16.
      • Step 2: Always multiply before you add. BEDMAS: Brackets, Exponents, Division/Multiplication, Addition/Subtraction!
    • Answer: 11

  3. [Easy] Ratio

    • EN: In a bag of marbles, there are 6 red and 9 blue. What is the ratio of red to blue?
    • FR: Dans un sac de billes, il y a 6 rouges et 9 bleues. Quel est le rapport rouge/bleu ?
    • Choices: A) 2:3 B) 3:2 C) 1:3 D) 6:15
    • Hint: A ratio compares two quantities. Simplify by dividing both numbers by their GCF! For example, 4 red to 8 blue = 4:8 = 1:2 (divide both by 4).
    • Steps:
      • Step 1: Try 8 red and 12 blue. GCF of 8 and 12 is 4. 8 ÷ 4 = 2, 12 ÷ 4 = 3. Ratio = 2:3.
      • Step 2: Divide both numbers in the ratio by their greatest common factor to simplify!
    • Answer: 2:3
