Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Toonies

    • EN: You have 3 toonies. How many toonies do you have in all?
    • FR: Tu as 3 pièces de 2 $. Combien de pièces as-tu en tout ?
    • Choices: A) 2 B) 3 C) 4 D) 5

Grade 2 Problems

  1. [Easy] Adding at the Skating Rink

    • EN: There are 23 skaters on the ice. 14 more join them. How many skaters are there now?
    • FR: Il y a 23 patineurs sur la glace. 14 autres se joignent à eux. Combien y a-t-il de patineurs maintenant ?
    • Choices: A) 35 B) 38 C) 37 D) 40
    • Hint: Add the tens first, then the ones.
    • Steps:
      • 20 + 10 = 30.
      • 3 + 4 = 7. So 30 + 7 = 37.
    • Answer: 37

  2. [Easy] Telling Time

    • EN: The clock shows 3 o'clock. Hockey practice starts in half an hour. What time does practice start?
    • FR: L'horloge indique 3 heures. La pratique de hockey commence dans une demi-heure. À quelle heure commence la pratique ?

    • Hint: Half an hour after 3:00 is 3:30.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 3:00.
      • Add 30 minutes: 3:30.
    • Answer: 3.30

  3. [Easy] Measuring a Scarf

    • FR: Une écharpe mesure 45 cm de long. Une autre écharpe mesure 32 cm de long. Laquelle est la plus longue et de combien de cm ?

    • Hint: Subtract the shorter length from the longer one.
    • Steps:

      • The first scarf is longer by 13 cm.
    • Answer: 13

    • EN: Maya has exactly 75 cents using only dimes and nickels. She has 5 dimes. How many nickels does she have?

    • EN: A hockey team orders 7 pucks for each of its 8 players. How many pucks does the team need in all?

    • Steps:

  3. [Easy] Area of a Rectangle

  5. [Medium] Long Division with Remainder

  8. [Medium] Decimal Addition
