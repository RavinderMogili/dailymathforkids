Grade 12 Problems

  1. [Easy] Permutations

    • EN: In how many ways can 4 students line up in a row?
    • FR: De combien de façons 4 élèves peuvent-ils se mettre en rang?
    • Choices: A) 12 B) 16 C) 24 D) 32
    • Hint: Number of ways = n! (n factorial). Try: 3 students → 3! = 3 × 2 × 1 = 6.
    • Steps:
      • 4! = 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 = 24.
    • Answer: 24

  2. [Easy] Combination

    • EN: How many ways can you choose 2 students from a group of 5?
    • FR: De combien de façons peut-on choisir 2 élèves parmi un groupe de 5?
    • Choices: A) 8 B) 10 C) 12 D) 20
    • Hint: Use C(n,r) = n! ÷ (r! × (n−r)!). Try: C(4,2) = 4!/(2!×2!) = 24/4 = 6.
    • Steps:
      • C(5,2) = 5! ÷ (2! × 3!) = (5×4) ÷ (2×1) = 20 ÷ 2 = 10.
    • Answer: 10

  3. [Easy] Derivative — Power Rule

    • EN: What is the derivative of f(x) = x²?
    • FR: Quelle est la dérivée de f(x) = x²?
    • Choices: A) f'(x) = x B) f'(x) = 2x C) f'(x) = 2 D) f'(x) = x²
    • Hint: Power rule: if f(x) = xⁿ, then f'(x) = n·xⁿ⁻¹. Try: f(x) = x³ → f'(x) = 3x².
    • Steps:
      • f(x) = x², n = 2.
      • f'(x) = 2 × x^(2−1) = 2x.
    • Answer: f'(x) = 2x

  4. [Easy] Polynomial — End Behaviour

    • Choices: A) f(x) → +∞ B) f(x) → −∞ C) f(x) → 0 D) f(x) → 2
    • Hint: The leading term controls end behaviour. A negative leading coefficient with odd degree goes to −∞ as x → +∞. Try: −x⁵ → as x → +∞, f → −∞.
    • Steps:
      • Leading term: −x³ (negative, odd degree).
      • As x → +∞, −x³ → −∞.
    • Answer: f(x) → −∞

  5. [Medium] Derivative — Rate of Change

    • EN: If f(x) = 3x² − 2x + 1, what is f'(x)?
    • FR: Si f(x) = 3x² − 2x + 1, quelle est f'(x)?
    • Choices: A) f'(x) = 6x − 2 B) f'(x) = 3x − 2 C) f'(x) = 6x + 1 D) f'(x) = 6x − 1
    • Hint: Differentiate term by term using the power rule. Try: f(x) = 4x² + 3x → f'(x) = 8x + 3.

      • d/dx (−2x) = −2.

      • f'(x) = 6x − 2.
    • Answer: f'(x) = 6x − 2

    • Answer: 3/8
