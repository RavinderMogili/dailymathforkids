Grade 1 Problems

  1. [Easy] Counting Hockey Pucks

    • EN: There are 7 hockey pucks on the ice. 3 more are added. How many pucks are there now?
    • FR: Il y a 7 rondelles de hockey sur la glace. On en ajoute 3 de plus. Combien de rondelles y a-t-il maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 9 B) 10 C) 11 D) 8
    • Hint: Count on from 7 by adding 3 more.
    • Steps:
      • Start at 7.
      • Count forward 3 more: 8, 9, 10.
    • Answer: 10

  2. [Easy] Maple Leaf Collecting

    • EN: Mia found 5 maple leaves in the morning and 6 in the afternoon. How many leaves did she find in all?
    • FR: Mia a trouvé 5 feuilles d'érable le matin et 6 l'après-midi. Combien de feuilles a-t-elle trouvées en tout?
    • Choices: A) 10 B) 9 C) 11 D) 12
    • Hint: Add the two groups of leaves together.
    • Steps:
      • Start with 5 leaves.
      • Add 6 more: 5 + 6 = 11.
    • Answer: 11

  3. [Easy] Pennies in a Jar

    • EN: Luca has 8 pennies. He gives 3 to his friend. How many pennies does Luca have left?
    • FR: Luca a 8 sous. Il en donne 3 à son ami. Combien de sous Luca a-t-il maintenant?
    • Choices: A) 4 B) 6 C) 5 D) 3
    • Hint: Take 3 away from 8.
    • Steps:
      • Start with 8 pennies.
      • Subtract 3: 8 − 3 = 5.
    • Answer: 5

  4. [Easy] Skip Counting by 2s

    • EN: Count by 2s: 2, 4, 6, 8, ___. What number comes next?

    • Hint: Each number goes up by 2.
    • Steps:
      • Look at the pattern: each number is 2 more than the last.
      • Add 2 to 8: 8 + 2 = 10.
    • Answer: 10

  5. [Medium] Snowflakes on a Window

  6. [Medium] Comparing Numbers

  8. [Medium] Skip Counting by 5s

    • EN: Count by 5s: 5, 10, 15, ___. What number comes next?

  8. [Medium] Telling Time — Half Hour

  9. [Hard] Spending Money

    • EN: Niko has 75¢. He buys a sticker for 38¢. How many cents does he have left?
    • FR: Niko a 75¢. Il achète un autocollant pour 38¢. Combien de cents lui reste-t-il?
    • Choices: A) 35 B) 39 C) 37 D) 33
    • Hint: Subtract 38 from 75. Watch the ones place carefully.
    • Steps:
      • 75 − 38: subtract ones: 15 − 8 = 7 (borrow from tens), tens: 6 − 3 = 3.
      • Wait, regroup: 75 − 38 = 37.
    • Answer: 37

  10. [Hard] Pattern Problem

    • EN: A pattern goes: 12, 17, 22, 27, ___. What number comes next?
    • FR: Un modèle: 12, 17, 22, 27, ___. Quel nombre vient ensuite?
    • Choices: A) 30 B) 32 C) 35 D) 29
    • Hint: Find how much the pattern increases each time.
    • Steps:
      • 17 − 12 = 5; 22 − 17 = 5; pattern adds 5 each time.
      • 27 + 5 = 32.
    • Answer: 32

Grade 4 Problems

  1. [Easy] Multiplication Facts

    • EN: A hockey team has 9 players. There are 7 teams in the league. How many players are there in total?

    • Hint: The first digit after the decimal point is in the tenths place.
    • Steps:
      • In 3.47, the digit 4 is in the tenths place.
      • Its value is 0.4.
    • Answer: 0.4

  3. [Easy] Area of a Rectangle

  9. [Hard] Multi-Step Area

    • EN: A rectangular park is 12 m long and 9 m wide. A square fountain 3 m × 3 m is inside. What is the area of the park outside the fountain?
    • FR: Un parc rectangulaire a 12 m de long et 9 m de large. Une fontaine carrée de 3 m × 3 m se trouve à l'intérieur. Quelle est l'aire du parc en dehors de la fontaine?
