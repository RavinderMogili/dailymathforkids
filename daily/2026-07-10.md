   - Hint: Expected value = Σ(outcome × probability) − cost — for a $3 game winning $8 half the time: E = 8 × 0.5 + 0 × 0.5 − 3 = 4 − 3 = $1.00.
   - Steps:
     - Win: net gain = $5 − $2 = $3, probability = 1/4.
     - Lose: net gain = $0 − $2 = −$2, probability = 3/4.
     - E = 3 × (1/4) + (−2) × (3/4) = 0.75 − 1.50 = −$0.75.
   - Answer: −$0.75

10. **[Hard] Vectors — Dot Product and Angle**
    - EN: Find the angle between vectors **u** = (1, 0) and **v** = (1, 1) using the dot product formula. (cos 45° ≈ 0.707)
    - FR: Trouve l'angle entre les vecteurs **u** = (1, 0) et **v** = (1, 1) en utilisant la formule du produit scalaire. (cos 45° ≈ 0,707)
    - Choices: A) 30°  B) 45°  C) 60°  D) 90°
    - Hint: cos θ = (u · v) ÷ (|u| × |v|) — for u = (1, 0) and v = (0, 1): dot product = 0, so θ = 90°.
    - Steps:
      - u · v = (1)(1) + (0)(1) = 1.
      - |u| = √(1² + 0²) = 1. |v| = √(1² + 1²) = √2.
      - cos θ = 1 ÷ (1 × √2) = 1/√2 ≈ 0.707.
      - θ = 45°.
    - Answer: 45°
