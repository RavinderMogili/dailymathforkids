# Math Topics by Grade (Based on Common Core Standards)

Use this as a guide when creating daily quiz questions.

---

## Grade 1 (5 problems)
- **Addition & Subtraction** — within 20 (word problems, counting on)
- **Place Value** — tens and ones, compare two-digit numbers
- **Counting** — count to 120, skip counting by 2s, 5s, 10s
- **Measurement** — compare lengths, tell time (hours & half-hours)
- **Geometry** — identify shapes (triangles, rectangles, circles)

## Grade 2 (5 problems)
- **Addition & Subtraction** — within 100, word problems
- **Place Value** — hundreds, tens, ones; compare 3-digit numbers
- **Measurement** — inches, centimeters, tell time to 5 minutes
- **Money** — count coins and bills, solve money word problems
- **Geometry** — recognize and draw shapes, partition into halves/thirds/fourths

## Grade 3 (8 problems)
- **Multiplication & Division** — facts within 100, word problems
- **Fractions** — understand 1/2, 1/3, 1/4; compare fractions; number line
- **Place Value** — round to nearest 10 or 100
- **Measurement** — perimeter, area, time intervals, liquid volume & mass
- **Geometry** — identify quadrilaterals, partition shapes into equal parts

## Grade 4 (8 problems)
- **Multi-digit Arithmetic** — multiply up to 4 digits by 1 digit, long division
- **Fractions** — equivalent fractions, add/subtract fractions with like denominators, mixed numbers
- **Decimals** — relate to fractions (tenths, hundredths)
- **Measurement** — unit conversions (km ↔ m, kg ↔ g), area & perimeter
- **Geometry** — angles (measure, classify), lines of symmetry

## Grade 5 (8 problems)
- **Decimals** — add, subtract, multiply, divide decimals
- **Fractions** — add/subtract unlike denominators, multiply fractions
- **Order of Operations** — parentheses, brackets, BEDMAS/PEMDAS
- **Volume** — rectangular prisms, unit cubes
- **Coordinate Plane** — plot points in first quadrant, ordered pairs

## Grade 6 (8 problems)
- **Ratios & Proportions** — understand ratios, unit rates, percentages
- **Integers** — negative numbers, number line, absolute value
- **Expressions & Equations** — write and evaluate simple expressions, one-step equations
- **Area & Surface Area** — triangles, quadrilaterals, 3D nets
- **Statistics** — mean, median, mode, range, dot plots, histograms

## Grade 7 (10 problems)
- **Proportional Relationships** — constant of proportionality, scale drawings
- **Operations with Rationals** — add, subtract, multiply, divide fractions & decimals
- **Expressions & Equations** — two-step equations, inequalities
- **Geometry** — area of circles, angle relationships, cross-sections of 3D shapes
- **Probability & Statistics** — simple probability, random sampling

## Grade 8 (10 problems)
- **Linear Equations** — slope, y-intercept, graphing lines, systems of equations
- **Functions** — define, compare, and graph functions
- **Exponents & Roots** — laws of exponents, square roots, cube roots, scientific notation
- **Pythagorean Theorem** — find missing sides, distance between points
- **Transformations** — translations, rotations, reflections, dilations

## Grade 9 (10 problems)
- **Linear Functions** — slope-intercept form, point-slope form, parallel/perpendicular lines
- **Quadratic Equations** — factoring, quadratic formula, graphing parabolas
- **Polynomials** — add, subtract, multiply polynomials
- **Inequalities** — solve and graph linear inequalities
- **Data Analysis** — scatter plots, line of best fit, correlation

## Grade 10 (10 problems)
- **Quadratics (advanced)** — completing the square, vertex form, discriminant
- **Trigonometry** — sin, cos, tan; SOH-CAH-TOA; solving right triangles
- **Circle Geometry** — arc length, sector area, central angles, tangent lines
- **Coordinate Geometry** — distance formula, midpoint, equation of a circle
- **Systems of Equations** — substitution, elimination, word problems

## Grade 11 (10 problems)
- **Functions** — domain & range, transformations, inverse functions
- **Exponential & Logarithmic** — growth/decay, log rules, solving log equations
- **Sequences & Series** — arithmetic and geometric sequences, summation
- **Trigonometric Functions** — unit circle, radians, graphs of sin/cos/tan
- **Financial Math** — simple & compound interest, annuities

## Grade 12 (10 problems)
- **Limits & Derivatives** — intro to calculus, rate of change, basic differentiation
- **Advanced Functions** — polynomial division, rational functions, asymptotes
- **Probability & Statistics** — combinations, permutations, expected value, normal distribution
- **Vectors** — addition, scalar multiplication, dot product
- **Proof & Logic** — mathematical induction, logical reasoning
